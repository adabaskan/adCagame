privacy policy – adCa

1. Introduction

This privacy policy is prepared to protect the privacy of users in the Android platform games developed by adCa. Our games do not collect any personal data from users.

2. Definitions

Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
User: Refers to the natural persons who use our games.

3. Data Collected

Our games do not collect, record, or share any personal information from users in any way.

4. Children's Privacy

Our games can be played by users aged 13 and over, as well as children under 13. Therefore, we give special importance to children's privacy. Since no personal data is collected in our games, children's privacy is protected.

5. Use of Cookies

Our games do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

6. Third-Party Services

Our games do not use advertising, analytics, or any other third-party services.

7. Third-Party Links

Our games may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of these sites.

8. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected, data security risks are minimal. However, the security of our users is important to us.

9. User Rights

Since no personal data is collected in our games, our users do not have any rights to claim regarding data under the Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK).

10. Acceptance of Policy

By using our games, you are deemed to have accepted this privacy policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy or our games, please contact us at the following email address:
adCagame@gmail.com

12. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are applied, the updated policy will be published on this page.
